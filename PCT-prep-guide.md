## 6. Permits & Compliance Checklist

| Permit | Coverage | Where | Cost | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## 7. Gear & Equipment Blueprint

### Core Kits

- **Navigation:** NST Guide web map + FarOut, compass (Trail-Compass concept), offline GPX on phones & Garmin.
- **Shelter/Sleep:** 3-season tent (Zpacks/Durston) or tarp, 20¬∞F quilts (EE Revelation style) or bags, NeoAir XLite NXT pads.
- **Cooking:** SOTO WindMaster or similar canister stove, titanium pot (750ml+), long spoon, bear hang kit.
- **Hydration:** Smartwater bottles + Sawyer Squeeze + CNOC Vecto 2L for dirty water collection.
- **Lighting:** Nitecore NU25 UL headlamp + backup LED safety beacons.
- **Safety:** Garmin inReach Mini 2, first-aid (Leukotape is mandatory), fire kit, whistle, bug repellent.

### Personal Packing Priorities

1. **Foot care kit:** Darn Tough socks (2-3 pairs), Leukotape for hot spots, Dirty Girl gaiters.
2. **Sun & bug defense:** Sun hoodie (Arcteryx/JollyGear), wide-brim hat, permethrin-treated clothing.
3. **Rain & Wind:** Lightweight rain shell (Montbell/Patagonia) + wind pants or rain skirt; pack liner.
4. **Comfort:** Camp shoes, book/podcasts for detox evenings, Nitecore NB10000 power bank.
5. **Finance:** cash + cards for Dunsmuir resupply; keep budgets transparent.
